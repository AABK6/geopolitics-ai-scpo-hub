MareNostrum, Barcelona

This is a real place, not an AI-generated picture: the MareNostrum data center, at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center, housed in a former chapel.

It captures one of the premises of the course: digital power appears borderless, but it is built from very material things — machines, energy, sites, states, and geopolitics. High-performance computing placed inside an older architecture of authority.
